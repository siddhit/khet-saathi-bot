AgriPulse Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

AgriPulse is a WhatsApp-based farm advisory tool built for use by farm supervisors. It is currently in a private pilot and is not available to the general public.

What we collect: WhatsApp messages and media (photos, voice notes) sent to the AgriPulse number by registered farm supervisors. We also collect the phone number associated with each message.

How we use it: Messages are processed to generate agricultural advice. We do not sell, share, or use this data for advertising.

Data retention: Message data is retained for the duration of the pilot to improve the advisory system. Supervisors may request deletion at any time.

Contact: For questions or data deletion requests, contact siddhits@gmail.com.
